About

DPS Introduction

DPS-JODHPUR

An oasis amidst the sands of Rajasthan, DPS Jodhpur stands tall with an ardent venture in the field of education and learning. Complimented with 15 acres of lush green environment conducive to an open view, the school campus is dotted with trees and an excellent infrastructure. It comprises of four blocks, three blocks dedicated to the three separate wings; Junior block for classes Nursery-III, Middle block for classes IV-VIII and Senior block for classes IX XII equipped with smart boards. There is a separate Lab and Library Block that houses Language Lab with smart board, four Science Labs viz Biology, Biotech, Chemistry and Physics, and labs for IT, Maths and Geography. There is a Resource Lab for teachers, a well equipped air conditioned Conference Hall, an Art Gallery and an air conditioned Auditorium with a seating capacity of about 600 students. The school has well resourced sports infrastructure with outdoor facilities including grounds for football and cricket, courts for basketball, volleyball, hand ball , tennis court, skating track and substantial indoor facilities for table tennis, carrom, chess, gym, yoga etc. In addition to the academic blocks, there is a residential block constructed within the campus. It comprises of three hostels, a mess block and staff quarters. There are separate hostels for boys and girls. A well stocked mess with modern gadgets, spacious dinning hall and a dedicated staff catering to the food and nutrition requirement of students is available round the clock.
